About Cats

Cats are small carnivorous mammals known for their independent and playful nature. They are popular pets around the world and come in various breeds, colors, and sizes.

Cat Breeds

There are many cat breeds, each with its own unique characteristics. Some popular cat breeds include Siamese, Persian, Maine Coon, and Bengal.

Fun Facts

  • Cats have retractable claws, which they use for grooming and climbing.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats can make over 100 different sounds.
